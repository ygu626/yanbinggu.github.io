Yanbing Gu

I am a fourth-year PhD student in the Department of Mathematics at Dartmouth College, advised by Dimitris Giannakis . I am interested in data-driven applications of dynamical systems and differential geometry, in particular, constructing spectral approximations for differential operators and non-parametric models for dynamical systems using machine learning and deep learning techniques.

I completed my Bachelor's in Mathematics, Applied Mathematics, and Statistics at Stony Brook University, during my undergrad, I was interested in geometric topology and topological graph theory, both of which I still hold very fond memories of.
