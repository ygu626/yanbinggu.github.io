Yanbing Gu

I am a fourth-year PhD student in the Department of Mathematics at Dartmouth College, advised by Dimitris Giannakis. I am interested in data-driven applications of dynamical systems and differential geometry, in particular, constructing spectral approximations for differential operators and conducting non-parametric modelling for dynamical systems using machine learning and deep learning techniques.

I completed my umdergraduate degree in mathematics, applied mathematics, and statistics at Stony Brook University, during which I was interested in geometric topology and topological graph theory. I still hold very fond memories for both areas, and try to keep up with their developments as much as I could.
